Our Impact

We have worked on improving the lives of over 25,000 young people through direct practice.

At Yadav Foundation, meaningful change starts with small, consistent actions. We uplift underprivileged communities, empower individuals through education, and promote sustainable development.


🌟 What We Do


🏥 Healthcare & Nutrition Access to basic healthcare should not be a luxury. We conduct: Free health camps and medical checkups in rural areas Nutritional support for children, pregnant women, and elderly citizens Menstrual hygiene awareness and distribution drives Mental health counseling and support services


👩‍👧 Women & Youth Empowerment We empower women and youth to become self-reliant through: Skill development programs (tailoring, digital literacy, handicrafts, etc.) Entrepreneurship training and financial literacy Awareness workshops on health, hygiene, and legal rights Leadership programs for young change-makers

🎓 Education for All We believe that education is a fundamental right, not a privilege. Our programs aim to: Provide access to quality education for underprivileged children Distribute books, uniforms, and school supplies Offer scholarships and tuition support Run after-school learning and mentoring sessions
